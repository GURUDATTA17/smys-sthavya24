About Hostel Day

"STHAVYA-2024" presents an exciting Inter-Hostel Competition from May 1 to May 12 at the Sri Madhwa Yuvaka Sangha. This three-day event on May 1, 11, and 12 features a lineup of sports and cultural competitions.

Registration forms for various contests are available under the Sports and Cultural section. Join us for thrilling competitions and a lively atmosphere as hostels vie for glory. Don't miss out on the fun and camaraderie at "STHAVYA-2024"!

Venue :Sri Madhwa Yuvaka Sangha Hostel premises.
